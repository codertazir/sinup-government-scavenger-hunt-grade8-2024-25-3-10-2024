End of an Era: Last Coal-Fired Power Plant Shuts Down

Britain’s last coal power plant at Ratcliffe-on-Soar in Nottinghamshire has closed down for good. This marks the end of the UK's long history with coal, which started 142 years ago. The government decided to shut it down to help the environment and fight climate change. Many people are happy about this change because it means cleaner air, but some workers are worried about losing their jobs.
